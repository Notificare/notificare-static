Notificare was founded by Joel Oliveira, Joris Verbogt and Robert Leefmans in the spring of 2012. They envisioned a platform that would bridge the gap between the virtual world and the ever growing number of connected devices.

Since day one Notificare has been powering applications with smarter push notifications. The unified messaging approach provides a unique solution that is truly cross-platform and goes beyond the mobile spectrum.

These tools have been helping companies, from all sizes, to create user centric experiences across iOS, Android and Web browsers.

We are based in Amsterdam, next to the EYE Film Institute Netherlands and surrounded by other amazing startups in our office at A LAB Amsterdam. You can always drop by our office, just give us call and yeah... don't forget to bring some candy. We love candy!
