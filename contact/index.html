Notificare was founded by Joel Oliveira, Joris Verbogt and Robert Leefmans in the spring of 2012. We envisioned a platform that would bridge the gap between the virtual world and the ever growing number of connected devices.

Since day one we been powering applications with push notifications, offering tools that help you engage and build a relationship with your customers. We will be keep releasing even more solutions to power your application with real-time updates that are truly rich and interactive.

We are based in Amsterdam and you can always drop by our office, don't forget to bring some candy.
