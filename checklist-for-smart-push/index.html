Checklist for Smart Push

A quick and powerful guide to a smarter push messaging approach.
Four steps to create a resourceful web or mobile push strategy.

Start today

Create User Profiles

Creating user profiles transforms your push messaging to a private line of communication with your users. Create personal messages that are as relevant as effective.

Segmentation

Categorise your users according to products you sell, services you provide or even a in-game level they reach. Send group messages to different target audiences and watch your engagement rates increase.

Tags

Use your app to categorise devices according to a sale you've made, a device specific feature or even a user preference. Put your app to work and automatically create groups with specific needs that you can address at any time.

Location Updates

If you already using Location Updates in your app then you might wanna take a couple of minutes to integrate Notificare. If you don't well then it's time to use our lightweight solution that transforms location into a relevant push.
