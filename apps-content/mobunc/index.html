Welcome

This app will be used during the Mobile Unconference to showcase some examples of mobile app technology

  • Send or schedule actionable messages with any type of content
  • Send messages based on user's location or when they enter or leave your stores and venues
  • Use Bluetooth Low-Energy devices to send contextual notifications when in proximity with your products or facilities
  • Monitor visits and visitors in all your locations
  • Discover and segment your audience
