Push from within

Take your existing CRM solution and turn it into a push messaging system. Send simple, one-to-one messages, broadcasts or criteria-based group messages. If your back-end can talk to an API, it can talk to us.

Use your business rules

Trigger push from events in your systems. Use the segmentation you already have in your user database for segmented push too. Combine your push messaging with existing solutions like email marketing campaigns.

Sync your users

Retrieve users that installed your app and add them to your back-end systems. Consolidate user data between your mobile app and other services.

Process feedback
