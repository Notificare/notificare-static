Rich push notifications

Just a simple alert, a fully designed HTML page or an elaborate map: we've got you covered! Our simple and intuitive web composer will get you up and sending messages in no time. It will guide you as you type, add content or actions, showing you what your message will look like during the whole process. Truly rich and interactive notifications at your fingertips.

Simple Alerts

Powerful when combined with actions. Significantly increase your app's retention by sending relevant updates to your users about new content in your app.
A great way to start a relationship.

HTML & Webpages

Beautifully designed messages? No problem. Create messages that can have text, links, images and all the benefits of HTML5. And if that is not enough, you can also point users to existing web pages.

Maps & Directions

Quickly create a map using our composers' extremely easy-to-use pin point map. Being able to show your brand new store on a map or give directions to the closest order pick up point is pretty sweet.

Images & Video

Include images or videos in your messages. Every Notificare account has enough storage space, so you can upload any media and quickly send your latest catalog or the aftermath movie of your latest party.

Full-blown actionable content

Go beyond traditional rich push notifications with our push actions. Transform your messages into campaigns, offer & redeem discounts, send questionnaires and surveys or simply ask a question. All your users' behavior and responses will be collected and shown to you, so you can measure how your messages perform. Access core functionality of a device, like the camera or default email and SMS composer. We like to call it smart push notifications.

One click response

Add simple actions to quickly collect your users opinions. Ask them how they liked your latest update. Or maybe even hear what your test users say about those new changes before you release them.

Ask for input

Easily prompt your users to write a review about your product. Have them take a picture and send it back to you. Notificare provides a private, two-way communication link between you and your users.

Access devices' native functionality

Actions also let you access other resources of your users' devices. You can ask you users to send a email using the default Mail client, compose an SMS or make a phone call.

Browsers & other apps

Notificare also plays well with other apps. Open a web page in the device's default browser? Or simply open your Facebook profile page? You can! We can open any other app directly from your push notifications.

Use your own code

Pushing your users to areas in your app or triggering methods in your code is also supported by our libraries. Basically, use any existing functionality of your application, from within your push notifications.

Integrate with your own API

Do you have back-end functionality or use a 3rd party system that can handle your users' responses? We made sure actions support requests to your existing API. We provide libraries for several back-end technologies.
