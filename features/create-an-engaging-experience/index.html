Rich push notifications

From a simple alert, a fully designed HTML file or elaborated maps... we've got you covered! Our simple and intuitive web composer can get you up and sending messages in no time. It will guide as you type, add content or actions, always showing how your message will look like during the whole process. Truly rich and interactive notifications at your fingertips.

Simple Alerts

Powerful when combined with actions. Increase significatively your app retention by sending relevant updates to your users about new content in your app.
A great way to start a relationship.

HTML & Webpages

Beatifully designed messages? No problem. Create messages that can have text, links, images and all the benefits of HTML5. And if that is not enough, you can also point users to existing webpages.

Maps & Directions

Quickly create a map using our composers' extremely easy-to-use pin point map. Showing your brand new store in a map or pointing directions to the closest pick up place of an order is pretty sweet.

Images & Video

Include images or videos in your messages. Every Notificare account has enough storage space so you can upload any media content and quickly send your latest catalog or the aftermath movie of your last party.

Full blown actionable content

Go beyond traditional rich push notifications with our push actions. Transform your messages into campaigns, offer & redeem discounts, send questionaires and surveys or simply ask a question. All your users behavioural data will be collected and shown to you so you can measure your messages performance. Access core functionality of a device, like the camera or default email and sms composer. We call it smart push notifications.

One click reponse

Add simple actions to quickly collect your users opinions. Ask them how they liked your latest update. Or maybe even hear what your test users say about those new changes before you release them.

Ask for input

Easily prompt your users to write a review about your product. Or to take a picture and send it back to you. Notificare provides a private two-way communication link between you and your users.

Access devices' native functionality

Actions also let you access other resources of your customers devices. You can ask you users to send a email using the default Mail client, compose a SMS or make a phone call.

Browsers & other apps

Notificare also plays well with other apps. Open a webpage in the devices' default browser? Or simply open your Facebook profile page? You can! We can open any other app directly from your push notifications.

Use your own code

Pushing your users to areas in your app or triggering methods in your code are also supported by our libraries. Basically, use any existing funcitonality of your application, in your push notifications.

Integrate with your own API

Do you have backend functionality or 3rd party systems that can collect your users responses? We made sure actions could support requests to your existing API and integrate seamlessly without having to change anything.
