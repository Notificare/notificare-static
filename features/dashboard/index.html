Blazing fast interface

One simple and powerful interface to manage all your apps. So simple, even your boss can use it.

Try for free for 30 days

Unlimited apps

Add as many applications as you need, all with their own collection of devices and user profiles. It's easy to keep development, acceptance and production separate by creating different applications for each of them. Applications can be selected from a searchable drop-down menu that's accessible from anywhere in the dashboard.

Manage your services

For unified push across all platforms, managing those services is a breeze. Upload your certificates for APNS and we take care of validating them and keeping track of expiration. Add your app ID in the AppStore, so we can direct your users to rating your app right from a push notification. Just enter your GCM sender ID and you'll be sending cross-platform messages in no time.

Measure your data

See how many users installed your app, and how many opted-in for push notification. See the distribution of your app among the platforms, versions, devices and languages. Keep track of your notifications sent, push-influenced app usage and more, per day, per month or per year.

Friendly message composer

Compose and preview your message from a single view. Instantly see how large your targeted audience is as you go. Pick your selection from a number of predefined, useful templates, ranging from simple alerts to questionnaires or rich media content with feedback.

Track your message performance

See how your users behave after you sent them a message. See your app's retention rate change over time. Gain immediate insight into the feedback your users are providing, the answers they give to questions, their happiness levels.

Find your audience

Make a relevant selection of users to target. Look for users around you or one of your locations. Segment and select by specific user characteristics like gender or age group. Send to devices with certain tags only, like language settings, skill level or user preference.
