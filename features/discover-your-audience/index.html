Get to know your users

We let you create personal, user-centered and user-driven experiences. Build a relationship based of on your users' interests, geographical position and online behaviour.

Start today

Personalized user experience.

Easily create a minimal user profile by mapping your user to websites like Facebook, Twitter, Gravatar or even your own existing authentication method. Profiles are our answer to a more personalized messaging experience. By creating a user profile your app is allowing Notificare to associate more than one device with a user. Not only can you send targeted messages to a particular user, you can now also single out their devices.

Direct Messaging

An open connection to your users' pockets. Easily access a user profile and send personalized messages. Get a complete overview of their responses, the devices they use and how often they interacted with your app.

Start a conversation

We provide your apps with a two-way communication line. Engage your users, start a conversation and measure the feedback. Identify who are your most active users and expand the ways they interact with your brand.

Segment your audience

Categorizing your users is important to keep a sane structure in how you target your audience. Segmentation can be combined with geo-location and provide you with a powerful search query that will cover all of your messaging needs. Easily target different groups of users with messages that are both relevant as they are effective.

Transform location into a relevant push

Our location services can gather enormous amounts of data in just 24 hours. When we mean enormous, we mean crazy valuable insights. We have also worked really hard to come up with a solution that is battery-life friendly but still efficiently makes use of GPS data and Bluetooth beacons to track your customers' location. Send messages when a customer enters one of your stores or takes a product out of your shelves.
