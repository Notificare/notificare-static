Get to know your users

We let you create personal, user-centered and user-driven experiences.
Building a relationship is all about communication.

Start today

Personalized user experience.

Easily create a minimal user profile by mapping your user to websites like Facebook, Twitter, Gravatar or even your own existing authentication method. Profiles are our answer to a more personalized messaging experience. By creating a user profile your app is allowing Notificare to associate more than one device with a user. Not only can you send targeted messages to a particular user, you can now also single out their devices.

Direct Messaging

An open connection to your users' pockets. Easily access a user profile and send personalized messages. Get a complete overview of their responses, the devices they use and how often they interacted with your app.

Start a conversation

Increase brand loyalty and engagement by listening to your users' feedback. Use your app to measure their happiness, trigger your users to open your app and drive the happiest to rate your app.

Segment your audience

Categorizing your users is important to keep a sane structure in how you target your audience. Segments and tags can be combined with geo-location and provide your app with a complex segmented query that will cover most of the cases you will ever think of. Grouping your customers, not only by the devices they are using but also based on their whereabouts, has never been this easy.

Transform location into a relevant push

Our location tracking capabilities can gather enormous amounts of data in just 24 hours. When we mean enormous, we mean crazy valuable insights. We have enhanced battery-friendly code, that enables us to efficiently track your customers' location.
Reach people with the right information, where they need it, when they need it.
