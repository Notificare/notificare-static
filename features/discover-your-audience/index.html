Personalized user experience.

Easily create a minimal user profile by mapping your user to websites like Facebook, Twitter, Gravatar or even your own existing authentication method. Profiles are our answer to a more personalised messaging experience. By creating a user profile your app is allowing Notificare to associate more than one device to a user. Not only you can send targetted messages to a particular user, you can now also single out their devices.

Direct Messaging

An open connection to your users pocket. Easily access a user profile and send personalised messages. Get a complete overview of their responses, the devices they use and how often they interacte with your app.

Start a conversation

Increase brand loyalty and engagement by listening to your users feedback. Use your app to measure their happiness, trigger your users back to your app and drive the happiest to rate your app.

Segment your audience

Categorizing your users is important to keep a sane structure in how you target your audience. Segments and tags can be combined with geo-location and provide your app with a complex segmented query that will cover most of the cases you will ever think of. Grouping your customers by what devices they are using or where they comune never been this humanly possible.
