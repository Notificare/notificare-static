Engaging experiences

Use the power of push notifications to fuel your customers' experience in your app.

  • Be relevant!

    Send messages to a group of users, a specific user or device, or even to users that are in a certain location.

  • Compose rich messages

    Direct your users to an existing web page, or, even better, compose your own HTML messages. Include any media file, use images, maps, etc. Just be creative!

  • Location aware

    Need to give directions or just show your retail stores on a map? Use our location tracking feature to point your users to the right place.

Two way communication

Stop shouting, start interacting with your users! Get the most out of push notifications.

  • Ask a question, get feedback!

    Measure your users' happiness level when you release new features. Trigger your biggest fans to rate your app. Or just ask them what they think of that awesome new product you just launched.

  • Get the most out of devices

    Through our SDK, you can access core functionality of your users' devices. Have them set up a call, send email or SMS, use the camera or simply ask them for input.

  • Use your own code

    Ask for input, then trigger your app or even already installed apps for more advanced functionality. Our API will also happily talk to your backend or existing third-party systems.

  • One push to rule them all

    Send your messages to any mobile platform, straight from the dashboard. Pick your audience based on user name, specific device, geographic location or send a broadcast. Choose a notification type, add actions, and preview before you send. Easy peasy!

  • Know thy users

    Look up users and get information about their devices. See what platforms, versions and languages are being used most for your app.

  • See what's happening

    Gain insight into the usage of your app in a glance. See statistics on registrations, active users and number of notifications sent. Analyze how fast users respond to notifications, the actions they take, the answers they give.

  • Integration is easy

    Our SDK lets you easily integrate Notificare into your app on iOS, Android or HTML5 apps. The straight forward implementation will have you up and running in no time. And if the dashboard doesn't satisfy your push needs, our REST API gives you the freedom to send notifications from your existing backend systems.
