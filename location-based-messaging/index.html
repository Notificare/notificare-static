Geo-Targeting

We made it extremely simple for your apps to use customers' location to categorize and send targeted messages that are both relevant as effective. Fine grain your target audience to specific neighborhoods. Learn and categorize your users by the places where they live or work. Easily search and select any position in the globe, define a radius and create a rich and interactive messages that reach your customers exactly where they are.

Geo-Fencing

With our SDK your app will be taking advantage of the best functionality that mobile platforms can offer. Remember... your users are on the move and we can give your application the power to reach them when the context is right. Never been this easy to send rich and interactive content when your users enter or leave a certain location. Greet your customers, give them offers or simply categorise by the places they are. A powerful solution that many industries can benefit from.

Micro-location & content advertising

With the increasing number of Bluetooth enable devices like smart watches, fitness bands or any other product that requires mobile devices to turn Bluetooth on, we provide you with solutions to harness yet another channel to create engaging experiences. Using Notificare Bluetooth low energy beacons, your app can now gather information or present content when your customers are physically in your store. Detect your users' proximity to display more information about the product they are looking at in their devices or any additional screens.
