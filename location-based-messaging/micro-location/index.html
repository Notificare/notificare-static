Micro-location with iBeacons

With the ever-increasing number of personal connected devices like smart watches and fitness bands, which require mobile devices to be Bluetooth-enabled, we provide you with solutions to harness yet another channel.

learn more

Using Notificare Bluetooth Low Energy beacons (iBeacons), your app can now gather information or present content when your customers are physically in your store or venue. Detect their proximity to display more information about the product they are looking at, either on their devices or on additional screens.

Proximity marketing

Operating off a 3V coin battery for up to 2 years, and strategically placed inside your store or venue, our Bluetooth smart iBeacons will enable you to create the ultimate engaging experience. Foster content discovery and speed up processes like coupon redemption, receipt delivery or pick-up reservations.

Customer journey

Our platform not only provides you with the tools you need to build your online mobile messaging campaigns, but now you can also extend that to your offline locations, engaging and influencing your customer's in-store buying behavior. Maximize your revenue by creating campaigns that attract and engage more customers than ever.

Conversion & Segmentation

Easily measure how your in-store content boosts sales or what areas of your venue engaged more visitors. Categorize users by measuring how long they dwell in certain areas of your store or the products they were most interested in and create even better offline campaigns that increase brand loyalty and retention rates.
