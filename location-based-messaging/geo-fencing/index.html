Geo-Fencing

With our Mobile Marketing Platform you will be able to take advantage of the best location based marketing functionality mobile platforms can offer. In our dashboard you will be able to define a geo-fence that sets a radius around a store or point location. Give your application the power to reach users when the context is right.

Track your customers

We made sure you can gather and monitor your users' location in an effective and non-intrusive way. By optimizing when and how you get constant locations updates, your application can now safely unleash this powerful source of data.

Leverage valuable information

Now that you have access to this enormous amount of data, we make sure you make the most out of it. Use it to segment your users according to the stores they visit or the time they spend in your venue. Take decisions based on metrics that really matter.

Quickly create geo-triggered messages using a simple and easy-to-use interface. Change the frequency of your messages, range or content. Easily manage location based messages that extend and maximize your offline campaigns.
